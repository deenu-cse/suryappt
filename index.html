üìèTypes of Solar Panels

Monocrystalline

  • High efficiency (17-22%)
  • Compact, durable
  • Best for small roofs
  • Higher cost

Polycrystalline

  • 15-18% efficiency
  • Cheaper option
  • Suits larger roofs

Thin-Film

  • 10-12% efficiency
  • Low-cost, lightweight
  • Ideal for commercial/low-light areas

Others

  • Bifacial: Dual-sided power
  • PERC: Higher efficiency
  • Solar tiles: Stylish but costly
